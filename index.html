What CAMP+ offers?

Camp+ offers users online store to let them quickly gear up and go to their next adventure in no time! We also properly guide user to must-have items for their next camping.

Our platform chooses only the best and safest locations, so you can enjoy your life at full, while being confident, that you are safe too!

Camp+ also has built-in weather assistant, which guides you to best time for your next camping, so bad weather won't ruin your adventure.

We offer also great camping spot near settlements, where you can park your vehicle and enjoy wildlife with other people.

Reasoning behind starting CAMP+ is our desire to make camping safe and accessable to everyone. We are commited to make peoples camping an unforgivable experience!
